AfriVest is a mobile application that aims to bridge the gap between youth entrepreneurs and mentors and investors

It provides a platform for entrepreneurs to showcase their ideas, connect with potential supporters, and access resources to help build a successful startup.

Afrivest provides a platform for young entrepreneurs to showcase their business ideas to a community of investors, mentors, and other entrepreneurs.
